## Context
_1–3 short paragraphs describing the problem, forces/constraints, and any assumptions that shaped the choice._

## Decision
_One crisp sentence with the choice you made, followed by a few bullets that make the decision specific and testable._

## Alternatives considered
_2–3 bullets comparing viable options (include “do nothing” if relevant). Note key pros/cons or why each was rejected._

## Rationale (decision drivers)
_Why this option won. Call out the trade-offs (e.g., delivery speed vs. operability, cost, risk, team skill, constraints)._

## Consequences
_What happens next: benefits, downsides/risks, follow-ups, and how this constrains or enables future decisions._

## Notes
_Links/evidence (commits, issues, release notes), references, and any open questions to revisit later._
